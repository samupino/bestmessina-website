BEST Messina is excited to announce that it will organize a BEST Summer Course in Messina **from July 26 to August 2, 2026**.

<!--truncate-->

## The course

The course, titled **“TRON: Rise of Autonomous Vehicles”**, will take place at the Department of Engineering of the University of Messina and will welcome up to 24 students selected from technical universities across Europe, together with students from the University of Messina.

BEST Courses are short academic experiences held in English, designed to complement university education with a practical and international approach. They bring together students from the BEST network for one or two weeks of lectures, laboratories, company visits and cultural activities.

### What participants will study

This year’s topic will focus on the technologies behind autonomous vehicles, with a path that starts from embedded systems and moves toward robotics and artificial intelligence.

Participants will explore Arduino and STM32 platforms, sensor data acquisition and filtering, control systems, real-time synchronization, ROS2, and the basics of machine learning applied to autonomous mobility. The programme is designed to connect theory with practice, helping students understand how hardware, software and decision-making systems interact in modern autonomous vehicles.

## A practical international experience

The academic programme will combine lectures by University of Messina academics, hands-on laboratory work and visits to local companies. These activities will give participants a concrete view of how the concepts studied in class are applied in research, engineering and industry.

At the same time, the course will offer an international environment where students can exchange perspectives, compare different academic backgrounds and improve their ability to work in English with peers from across Europe.

## Promoting Messina in Europe

Beyond the academic programme, the course will also be an opportunity to promote Messina, Sicily and the University of Messina within the BEST network. Participants will discover the culture, landscape and traditions of our territory through social activities and guided visits, in line with BEST’s mission of “promoting Europe among Europeans”.
